Learning Tools for Music Students

Seegmund Music Lab provides learning resources for music students and learners. You can use it for exam preparation, such as music theory problem sets and ear training apps, as well as for daily practice.

Currently, we focus on basic functionalities, but future additions will include harmony theory tools, sight-singing exercises, and piano learning support tools.
