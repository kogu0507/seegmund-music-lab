Privacy Policy

Seegmund Music Lab (hereinafter referred to as "this site") respects the privacy of users and pays utmost attention to the protection of personal information. This privacy policy describes the types of information collected by this site, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Article 1: Information Collected

This site may collect the following personal information:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Approximate regional information inferred from IP address
  • Cookie information (session cookies, persistent cookies, etc.)
    • Improving site performance
    • Advertising (advertising cookies)
    • Analysis of site usage (analysis cookies)
  • Access logs
  • Device information (device type, OS, browser type, language settings, etc.)
  • Location information (prefecture-level information inferred from IP address)
  • Browsing history (page browsing history within the site, time spent, links clicked, etc.)

Handling of Personal Information of Minors

This site does not intend to collect personal information directly from children under the age of 13. If it is found that personal information of a child under the age of 13 has been collected without parental consent, it will be deleted immediately.

If a minor uses this site, **please use it under the supervision of a parent or legal guardian**.

  • Review browser privacy settings and delete unnecessary cookies
  • Use parental controls to manage appropriate site usage
  • Disable personalized ads on Google's ad settings page

For more information, please refer to the information provided by each browser and device.

If you wish to delete your personal information, please contact us using the method described in Article 8, "Contact Us".

Article 2: Use of Cookies

This site uses cookies to improve the user experience.

  • Improving site performance
  • Advertising (advertising cookies)
  • Analysis of site usage (analysis cookies)

Article 3: Use of Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: For advertising
  • Google Analytics: For site usage analysis

Article 4: Data Protection

This site uses SSL encryption to appropriately protect collected personal information.

Article 5: User Choices

Users can disable cookies through their browser settings.

Article 6: User Rights

Users can request disclosure, correction, addition, deletion, suspension of use, erasure, or cessation of provision to third parties regarding their personal information held by this site. For how to make a request, please contact us using the method described in Article 8, "Contact Us".

Procedure for Exercising Rights

When making a request, you may be asked to provide the following information to confirm your identity (or that of your agent):

  • Name
  • Contact information (email address, etc.)
  • Request details (disclosure, correction, deletion, etc.)
  • Identification documents (copies of driver's license, passport, etc.)

We will respond to requests appropriately in accordance with laws and regulations, and will usually respond within 14 days of receiving the request.

For personal information of minors, their parent or legal guardian can also exercise the above rights using the method described in Article 8, "Contact Us".

Article 7: Changes to the Privacy Policy

This site may update the privacy policy from time to time.

Article 8: Contact Us

For inquiries regarding the privacy policy, please contact us here.
